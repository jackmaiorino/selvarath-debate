What we can bank

  • The limited-verification harm is real for a strong judge and not the default-to-B parse artifact.
  • Banked (narrow): in the original pilot implementation, a few oracle calls worsened the 70B judge's accuracy (Δfew +7.2pp, CI [4.6,10.2]). Everything interpretive — mechanism split, deep-myopia share, confidence trend — is reset pending the fixed-harness re-judge.
  • The 8B judge is unusable for the headline (Position-B bias); budget-20 "recovery" is underpowered.
